Whether it's enhancing the security of a property with our sliding gates or adding elegance to a building with our customized windows and balconies, we are committed to delivering excellence in every aspect of our work.


Main doors

Sliding doors are designed to save space and provide easy access. They operate on a track system and slide horizontally.

  • Material Options: Aluminum, glass, and steel materials of all kinds of gauge.
  • Applications: Ideal for both residential, commercial spaces and office partitions.
  • Customization: Can be customized in terms of size, color, and glass type (e.g., frosted, clear, tinted).

Windows:

We design and manufacture windows of different types and styles, ensuring energy efficiency, security, and aesthetic appeal.

Types:

  • Casement Windows: Hinged on one side and open outward.
  • Sliding Windows: Move horizontally on a track.
  • Awning Windows: Hinged at the top and open outward.
  • Fixed Windows: Do not open, used for aesthetic or light purposes.
  • Materials: Typically aluminum, steel.

Opening and Sliding Gates

Our sliding gates combine robust materials with innovative designs, providing security and access control convenience for residential and commercial properties.

Types:

  • Swing Gates: Open inward or outward on hinges.
  • Sliding Gates: Move horizontally along a track.
  • Materials: Steel and aluminum.
  • Customization: Can be designed with various patterns and finishes; can include automation features for remote operation.

Wall Fences

Wall fences are used for security, privacy, and aesthetic purposes:

Types:

  • Wrought Iron Fences: Decorative and strong.
  • Chain Link Fences: Economical and functional.
  • Customization: Height, design, and material options to suit specific needs.

Balconies

Balconies add outdoor space and can be designed to enhance the aesthetics of a building:

Types:

  • Decorative railings that do not project from the building.
  • Extend out from the building without support underneath.
  • Structural Balconies: Supported by columns or brackets.
  • Materials: Stainless steel, glass, steel or a combination.
  • Features: Safety railings, non-slip surfaces, custom designs.

Kiosks

Kiosks are small, standalone structures used for various commercial purposes:

Types:

  • Retail Kiosks: For selling goods or services in high-traffic areas.
  • Information Kiosks: Provide information and assistance.
  • Materials: Metal, glass, and durable composites.
  • Features: Customizable design, signage options, and security features.

    • Structural Components:

    We fabricate structural components for various applications, ensuring strength, durability, and precision.

    Types:

    • Beams: Support structures and distribute loads.
    • Columns: Vertical supports that carry the weight of the structure.
    • Brackets: Provide additional support and stability.

Decorative Metal Artwork

These are artistic creations made from metal that can be displayed as standalone pieces or integrated into architecture.

Types:

  • Wall Art: Metal sculptures or panels that can be mounted on walls for decorative purposes.
  • Sculptures: Freestanding or wall-mounted metal sculptures that can be abstract or themed.
  • Mosaic Panels: Metal panels with mosaic patterns, often incorporating different colors and textures.
