    • Gazebos

    Definition and Use:

  • Gazebos are freestanding, open-sided structures with a roof, providing shade and shelter.
    They are commonly used for outdoor gatherings, relaxation, and aesthetic enhancement.

    • Types:

  • Traditional Gazebos: Often hexagonal or octagonal with intricate woodwork or metalwork.
  • Modern Gazebos: Sleek designs with clean lines and minimalistic features, often made from metal or composite materials.
  • Portable Gazebos: Lightweight and easy to set up and dismantle, often used for temporary events.

    • Features:

  • Materials: Wood, metal (aluminum or steel), or composite materials.
  • Roof Options: Shingle, metal, or fabric.
  • Customizable: Can include features like built-in seating, lighting, or curtains.

    • Applications:

  • Residential: Gardens, patios, and pool areas.
  • Commercial: Parks, event spaces, and outdoor cafes.


    • Pergolas

    Definition and Use:

    Pergolas are structures with a framework covered by an open lattice or roof, providing partial shade and support for climbing plants.

    Types:

  • Traditional Pergolas: Made from wood, often with classic designs and finishes.
  • Modern Pergolas: Featuring sleek, geometric designs and materials like aluminum or steel.
  • Attached Pergolas: Attached to a building, creating an extended outdoor living space.

    • Features:

  • Materials: Wood, metal (aluminum or steel), or composite materials.
  • Roof Options: Lattice, fabric coverings, or retractable roofs.
  • Customizable: Can include built-in seating, lighting, or climbing plant support.

    • Applications:

  • Residential: Gardens, patios, and walkways.
  • Commercial: Restaurant patios, parks, and garden centers.

    • Kiosks

  • Information Kiosks: Providing maps, guides, or interactive information.
  • Service Kiosks: For ticketing, registration, or customer service.
  • Materials: Metal, wood, or composite materials.
  • Designs: Customizable with signage, counters, and storage.
  • Portability: Can be designed for permanent or mobile use.

    • Applications:

  • Commercial: Shopping malls, parks, and event venues.
  • Public Spaces: Tourist information, ticketing, and concessions.
